There are six 16 pins HE14 connectors with 3 different configurations (bad design !).
Each connectors use 4 pins for the RGB LED.

Seconds, minutes, hours :

  1 - - 0
  3 - - 2
  5 - - 4
  7 - - 6
  9 - - 8
 HV - - HV
  G - - B
GND - - R

10 seconds, 10 minutes :

  1 - - 0
  3 - - 2
  5 - - 4
 HV - - HV
    - -
    - -
  G - - B
GND - - R

10 hours :

  1 - - 0
    - - 2
    - -
 HV - - HV
 I1 - - HV I1
 I2 - - HV I2
  G - - B
GND - - R

I1 and I2 are the separators IN-3 tubes.
